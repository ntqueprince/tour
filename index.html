Route Guide

How to Reach

Simple step-by-step route: Delhi se Dehradun, phir Dehradun se Mussoorie.

1

Delhi to Dehradun

Kashmiri Gate ISBT se night govt bus lo. Yeh budget-friendly aur time-saving option hai.

2

Where to Take Bus

Bus stand par Dehradun counter/bay confirm karo. Ticket online ya counter se le sakte ho.

3

Dehradun to Mussoorie

Dehradun ISBT/nearby taxi point se shared taxi ya local bus lo. 1.5–2 hours ka scenic route hai.

Important

Travel Tips

  • Carry light luggage; hill roads par easy movement hota hai.
  • Share taxi to save money and avoid private cab cost.
  • Avoid expensive cafes if strict budget follow karna hai.
  • Book night bus to save time and one night stay cost.
Why Mussoorie

Special Things

  • Known as the “Queen of Hills”.
  • Beautiful mountain views almost everywhere.
  • Cool weather, especially evenings mein.
  • Famous Mall Road vibe for walks, snacks and photos.
